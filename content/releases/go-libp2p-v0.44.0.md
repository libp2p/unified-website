## Highlights
## Address Pipeline:

- Observed Address Manager has been moved out of identify to its own package, `github.com/libp2p/go-libp2p/p2p/host/obsaddrs`
- <g-emoji class="g-emoji" alias="warning">⚠️</g-emoji>  Identify Service doesn't support the `DisableObservedAddrManager` Option. The top level libp2p option `DisableIdentifyAddressDiscovery` works as it used to.
